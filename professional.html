Professional Experience

This page documents my professional experience within Data Science.

Data Science Intern

OhanaHealth - Job Recommendation System.

Jun - Aug 2020

In the summer of 2020, I worked as a data science intern at OhanaHealth. I was tasked with the responsibility of developing a recommendation system to automate the process of matching candidates to jobs they would be the best fit for. I was also tasked with analyzing user segments to better understand the user base.

  • Boosted customer satisfaction by 50% by designing and developing the MVP of a knowledge-and-content-based recommendation system to personalize the jobs displayed to each user based on their backgrounds.
  • Scraped the web for jobs under different industrial domains and used the data to train and fine tune a text classification model (RoBERTa) to classify each job based on the job description.
  • Analyzed customer backgrounds through topic modeling on resume text data and used topic distribution per resume to create user segments and personalize jobs recommendations.
