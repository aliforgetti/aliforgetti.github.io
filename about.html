Who I Am

I am a Data Science graduate student currently completing my Masters of Science in Data Science at Vanderbilt University (Graduating 2021). Over the course of my degree and through the projects I have been involved in, I have developed skills within predictive modeling systems, recommendation systems, language technology, and health decision support tools. You can go through an exhaustive list of my involvements on the portfolio page of this website.

I envision a world where data is ubiquitously used to shine light upon human behavioral patterns to improve collective human wellbeing and health outcomes. I wish to be a part of bringing this vision to reality.
